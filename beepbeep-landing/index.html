About BeepBeep!

Application for a safer trip that allows your family and friends to know what happens to you and where are you when you are traveling

Localization

BeepBeep! allow to comunicate your position to those who care about you.

Phone Status

Also gives to your virtual travel companions the status of your phone, like battery and signal.

SMS comunication

In app notifications and SMS notifications, even with out signal or battery.

History

Complete track of changes in trip, full history of all your travels.
